# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue, do not open a public issue.

- Email: `security@skia.local`
- Subject: `Skia-Status Security Report`
- Include: affected component, reproduction steps, impact, and any suggested mitigation.

## Response Targets

- Initial acknowledgment: within 2 business days
- Triage decision: within 5 business days
- Remediation timeline: based on severity and operational risk

## Disclosure Policy

- Please allow coordinated disclosure before publishing details.
- We may request validation on fix candidates before release.
- Critical issues are prioritized over feature work.

## Scope

This policy applies to the status site, incident publishing pipeline, and associated operational documentation in this repository.
